About

Buying some beautiful stationery can be one of the best ways to re-engage with the physical world and reboot your imagination. Plus, you don't even need to spend a lot of money to adorn your desk with gorgeous, designer-led products. Some of the world's most alluring bespoke stationery can be surprisingly affordable if you know where to look.Dhanda Stationers store is one of the famous shops in Panipat because it sells stationery at a wholesale rate of 20-30% cheaper than retail price. Moreover, they have different items like art material, drawing material, architecture material, and engineering instruments.

For this reason, the store is famous primarily among college students. Besides this, they have oil paints, brushes, spray cans, and more.It is a big store offering a variety of paints, papers, pens, colors, notebooks, gift items, etc. There is one item, i.e., sub-board, which you need to tell them in advance and the size you need, and they will order for you. From ribbons colored pens to glitter pens, you will get everything here. So go and check out this fantastic stationery shop in Panipat, Model Town.
